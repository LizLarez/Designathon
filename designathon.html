Ideation

Two heads are better than one. Bring your team together and let your ideas flow freely. That's how you start the design thinking process into a problem-solving solution.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Bring your ideas to life. Sketch, sketch, sketch! Build, build, build! Creating a user persona may help your ideas reach that extra mile!

Don't forget: this is an iterative process!

User Testing

The best way to understand the strengths and flaws of your design is to present it to who is going to potentially use it. Try to detach emotions from the design and look at the feedback as objectively as possible.
