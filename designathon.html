What are we going to do?

IDEATE

Based on Google's Design Sprint, start with sharing fresh new ideas with your team. Learn about your users, build a user journey map, define goals and targets, define features.

CREATE

Bring your ideas to life! Draw solutions, learn from them and select the best approach. You should have the first sketch of your functional prototype by the end of this step.

Don't forget: this is an iterative process!

TEST

The best way to understand the strengths and flaws of your design is to present it to who is going to potentially use it. Test with real users and get valuable feedback. Learn and adjust your storyboard. You should have a final functional prototype by now.

WIN

We have prizes for all participants and even more special prizes for the top groups. All project submissions will be assessed by a very excited juri. Make sure you have FUN!
